# Product Profile — Examiner AI

## Core Details
* **Product Name**: Examiner AI
* **Audience**: Students and Educators practicing CBSE curriculum exam papers.
* **Aesthetic Register**: **Swiss Minimal** (Clean, high-contrast, structured layout with 8pt grid, minimal borders, and a solid primary accent).
* **Variance Dial**: 3 (Strict layout, clean cards, no visual surprises)
* **Motion Dial**: 4 (Subtle hover states, snappy transitions, and a 2D interactive Dino loading animation)
* **Density Dial**: 7 (Moderately dense to support tabular question lists, badges, and code blocks)

## Brand Identity & Color Accents
* **Base Register**: Monochrome base (`#faf9f6` canvas background, `#0f172a` primary ink text).
* **Accent**: Single amber/yellow accent (`#d97706`) for highlights, progress, and primary actions.

## Anti-References & Style Constraints
* **No AI Tells**:
  * No indigo or purple gradients.
  * No three equal-height cards layout (use structured list groups or asymmetrical cards).
  * No glassmorphism (`backdrop-blur` content overlays).
  * No section-number eyebrows (e.g. `01 — Features`).
  * No pure black `#000` or pure white `#fff` for content backgrounds (use sand/ink off-colors).
